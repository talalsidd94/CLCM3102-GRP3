Tour Package 1 - Sydney, Australia

In seven days, you can see the highlights of three Australian cities, with day trips from each. Pair Melbourne, Sydney, and Cairns, for example, and you can easily visit the Blue Mountains and Great Barrier Reef or head to Western Australia, basing yourself in Exmouth and Perth, where you'll swim with whale sharks and snorkel off Rottnest Island. Other options featured below include a Whitsundays island retreat, Outback exploration in Uluru, and a family-friendly Gold Coast adventure.

Cost: 100 $

Tour Package 2 - Paris, France

Planning a ten-day trip to France? If variety is what you're after, start (or end) your trip with three days in Paris, and split the rest of your time between two other areas. Provence pairs well with the French Riviera, or opt to chase the wines of Bordeaux with castles and history in the Loire Valley. That said, each region and city in France contains multitudes, and you can easily spend your whole trip exploring one place: travel by train through the French Alps, hopping from town to charming town, or stick to the beautiful Provençal countryside. Enjoy the majestic beauty that is southern France.

Cost: 500 $

Tour Package 3 - New York, USA

In a five-day trip to New York City, you can experience a rich array of attractions. Begin by exploring Central Park and the MET, then venture down 5th Avenue and visit Rockefeller Center. Day two takes you to the Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, and the Financial District. On day three, delve into Museum Mile and ascend the Empire State Building. Day four explores Harlem, including the Apollo Theater and Columbia University, while day five brings you to Greenwich Village, Chelsea Market, and the High Line. Be sure to leave room for serendipitous discoveries.

Cost: 300 $

Tour Package 4- Maldives, South Asia

During a seven-day trip to the Maldives, you can immerse yourself in the ultimate tropical paradise. Spend your days snorkeling or diving in the crystal-clear waters, exploring vibrant coral reefs and encountering diverse marine life. Relax on pristine white-sand beaches, indulge in spa treatments, and savor exquisite seafood. Take a sunset cruise or engage in water sports like kayaking and windsurfing. Discover local culture by visiting islands, exploring markets, and experiencing traditional dance performances. Don't miss the opportunity to stay in an overwater bungalow for a truly magical experience.

Cost: 200 $

Tour Package 5 -Switzerland, Europe

A five-day trip to Switzerland promises a blend of breathtaking natural beauty and cultural experiences. Begin in Zurich, exploring its Old Town and the stunning Lake Zurich. Move on to Lucerne, where the Chapel Bridge and Lion Monument await. Day three takes you to Interlaken for outdoor adventures and a visit to Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe". Day four immerses you in the charm of Montreux and a cruise on Lake Geneva, while day five lets you explore Geneva's international flair, including the United Nations headquarters. Don't forget to savor Swiss chocolates and scenic train journeys.

Cost: 300 $

Tour Package 6 - Italy, Europe

In a 10-day trip to Italy, you can immerse yourself in a rich tapestry of cultural and historical experiences. Begin in Rome, exploring the Colosseum, Vatican City, and Trevi Fountain. Continue to Florence for Renaissance art at the Uffizi Gallery and the iconic Duomo. Discover the enchanting canals of Venice before heading to the Amalfi Coast for breathtaking coastal views. Explore Pompeii's ancient ruins and enjoy the culinary delights of Naples. In Tuscany, savor wine in Chianti and visit medieval towns like Siena. Conclude in Milan, experiencing its fashion and the grandeur of the Duomo. Throughout, relish Italian cuisine, from mouthwatering pasta dishes to gelato.

Cost: 400 $
